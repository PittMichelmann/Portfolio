Short Movie: Loop

A German short movie created for our Mediainformatics course. Summary: A guy gets to repeat the best day of his life over and over again. Over the course of many cycles he realizes, that he is missing something and instead of repeating his day chooses to break the cycle by rescuing a woman who otherwise would have died in a car crash.
